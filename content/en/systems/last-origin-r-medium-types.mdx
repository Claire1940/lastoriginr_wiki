### Top Tier Unit Analysis

**Alexandra**
Alexandra has seen significant improvements following recent balance updates. Her passive is now much easier to trigger, allowing her to contribute reliable electric-based AoE damage. Beyond her combat prowess, she provides an EXP boost to the team, making her the premier choice for leveling up newer units in your roster.

**Sowan**
Sowan remains a staple for players who value follow-up attacks. Her armor penetration allows her to shred through high-defense targets that would otherwise stall your progress. While she has been slightly overshadowed by newer power-creep units, her reliability in Light-focused teams is still unmatched.

**Khan**
Do not let her "Attacker" tag fool you; Khan is one of the most effective evade tanks in the game. When equipped with her exclusive "Dust Storm" gear, she becomes nearly untouchable. She is the cornerstone of many high-level compositions, providing both protection and offensive pressure.

  <Step num="1" title="Analyze Enemy Formation">
