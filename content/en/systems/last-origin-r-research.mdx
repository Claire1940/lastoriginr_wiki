### Key Combat Strategies

| Strategy Type        | Description                                                         | Example                                                          |
|----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Focus Fire**       | Concentrating attacks on a single high-priority enemy to eliminate it quickly. | Targeting enemy healers or main damage dealers first.            |
| **Area Control**     | Using skills to restrict enemy movement or damage multiple foes.      | Placing units to block paths or using AoE attacks on grouped enemies. |
| **Buff/Debuff Cycling** | Applying positive effects to allies and negative effects to enemies consistently. | Using a support unit to boost attack, then a debuffer to lower enemy defense. |
| **Positional Advantage** | Moving units to optimal grid squares for attack range, evasion, or protection. | Placing a ranged unit behind an obstacle or a tank in a choke point. |
| **Turn Manipulation** | Utilizing skills that affect turn order or grant extra turns.       | Speed-boosting an ally or delaying an enemy's turn.             |
