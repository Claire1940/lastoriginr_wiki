### Understanding Set Bonuses

Many high-tier **Last Origin gear** pieces belong to sets. Equipping multiple pieces from the same set grants powerful additional bonuses.

| Set Bonus Type    | Effect                                     | Ideal Units                             |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------- |
| **Attack Set**    | Increases overall Attack by a percentage   | Damage Dealers, Aggressors              |
| **Defense Set**   | Boosts Defense and HP                      | Defenders, Tanks, Support Units         |
| **Speed Set**     | Increases Action Points (AP) and turn order| Utility Supports, Debuffers             |
| **Critical Set**  | Enhances Critical Hit Rate and Damage      | High-ATK Damage Dealers, Snipers        |
| **Recovery Set**  | Improves healing received or given         | Healers, Self-sustaining Units          |

<Callout type="info" title="Synergy is Key">

</Callout>
