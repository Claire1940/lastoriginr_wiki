Proper stat allocation is crucial for maximizing your team's performance. Follow these guidelines for the Mongoose Team:

### Basic Combat Guide for Beginners

Last Origin's combat system allows for two core playstyles: Auto/Semi-Auto and Manual.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Auto/Semi-Auto Combat">
    - **Ideal for beginners** and AFK farming.
    - Teams fight independently without manual skill control.
    - Excellent for clearing early floors and resource farming.
    - Semi-auto teams like "Power of the Horde" activate quickly, dealing consistent damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manual Combat">
    - Requires active skill order selection and target priorities.
    - Unlocks full team potential for tough endgame content.

    - Avoid as a new player; master auto-farm first.
  </Card>

Learn basic counter rules: tribal teams struggle against Glutton and Centurion; fire damage is ineffective on ice bosses; lightning teams should avoid Lightning Bombers. Start with semi-auto teams to farm resources on the first 30 floors, master auto combat, then transition to manual builds as you progress.
