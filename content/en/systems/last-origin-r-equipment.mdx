<Section icon={Shield} title="Fundamentals of last origin r equipment">

Understanding the mechanics of **last origin r equipment** is the cornerstone of progressing from the early story stages into the grueling endgame. In this turn-based strategy RPG, equipment functions as a modular stat-booster that can compensate for a unit's natural weaknesses or amplify their core strengths. Unlike many other gacha games, the most vital gear in the early to mid-game is handed to players for free through missions and Tactical Training.

Every Bioroid and AGS unit has slots for different types of equipment, including Chips, OS (Operating Systems), and Special Gear. Chips generally provide raw stat boosts like Attack (ATK), Critical Rate (CRIT), and Evasion (EVA). The OS usually modifies combat behavior or provides niche bonuses like increased damage against specific enemy types. Special Gear offers unique utility, such as energy regeneration or defensive shields.

| Equipment Type | Primary Function | Best Used On |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chips** | Raw stat increases (ATK, CRIT, EVA) | All units based on role |
| **OS** | Combat behavior & niche damage | DPS units for specific counters |
| **Special Gear** | Utility, Shields, Energy | Tanks and Support units |

<Section icon={Target} title="Critical Stat Thresholds and Gear Benchmarks">

To succeed in the mid-game and early endgame, your **last origin r equipment** must help your units meet specific stat "breakpoints." If you fall below these numbers, your team will likely fail auto-battle loops, leading to wasted resources and time.

| Stat Metric | Recommended Threshold | Why It Matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical Rate** | **100%** | Ensures consistent damage and triggers specific passives |
| **Accuracy** | **300%** | Necessary to hit high-evasion enemies in later stages |
| **Armor Pen** | **90%+** | Allows damage to bypass heavy enemy defenses |
| **Evasion** | **Max Possible** | Critical for Evasion Tanks like Pinto to survive |

Beyond raw stats, you must consider the synergy between your team's passives and their gear. For example, the Mongoose Team relies on chain attacks and assists. If your main dealer lacks the accuracy to hit a target, the entire chain of assist attacks will fail to trigger, drastically reducing your total damage per turn.
