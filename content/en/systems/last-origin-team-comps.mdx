<Section icon={Shield} title="Essential Resource Allocation for New Players">
Effective resource management is critical when building your **Last Origin team comps**. New players start with valuable assets like 300k resources, 2 Black Books, and β chips. Using these wisely will prevent common mistakes and accelerate your progression.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Common Mistakes">

</Callout>

### Key Resource Usage Guide

| Resource        | Priority Usage                                  | Avoid                                    |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Resources**   | Offline recovery, equipment enhancement         | Heavy construction (inefficient early)   |
| **Black Books** | Late-game meta units (No.85 Invincible Dragon)  | Random units; consider T-14 Miho or No.7 Scissors Lise if absolutely necessary early |
| **Equipment**   | Upgrade free β chips (+10), equip CRIT/ATK chips on DPS | Ignoring chip upgrades                   |
| **Cores**       | Blue common cores for early links               | Gold rare cores on non-top-tier units (save for No.2 Lavata, No.85 Invincible Dragon) |
| **Tuna Cans**   | Expanded Squad Deployment Bays (aim for 5), character slots, equipment slots | Gacha skins, waiting room slots (clear inventory regularly) |

### Basic Combat Strategies

  <Step num="1" title="Master Auto/Semi-Auto">

  <Step num="2" title="Understand Team Composition">

  <Step num="3" title="Learn Counter Rules">

  <Step num="4" title="Prepare for Manual Combat">
