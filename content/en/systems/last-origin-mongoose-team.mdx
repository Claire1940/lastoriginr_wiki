<Section icon={Target} title="Stat Allocation and Gear Thresholds">

In Last Origin R+, raw levels are secondary to correct stat distribution. A level 100 unit with poorly allocated points will fail where a level 80 unit with optimized stats succeeds. For the **last origin mongoose team**, you must hit specific "breakpoints" to ensure 100% reliability during auto-farming.

| Stat Type | DPS (Miho/Bulgasari) | Evasion Tank (Pinto) | Defense Tank (Draco) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical Rate** | **100% (Mandatory)** | 0% | 0% |
| **Accuracy** | 300% - 350% | 150% | 200% |
| **Evasion** | Base only | **All Points (Max)** | Base only |
| **Defense / HP** | Base only | Base only | **Balanced Split** |

The most critical rule for any damage dealer is reaching **100% Critical Rate**. This includes the bonuses from your equipment (chips/scopes) and passive skills. Once you hit 100% Crit, every single remaining point must go into **ATK**. For tanks like Pinto, any point not spent on Evasion is a wasted point, as her survival depends entirely on not being hit.

<Callout type="info" title="Equipment Priority">
Prioritize upgrading your **β Chips** (Attack, Crit, and Evasion) to +10. These free items provided early in the game are powerful enough to carry the Mongoose Team through Chapter 6.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Resource Management: Tuna Cans and Black Books">

New Commanders often make the mistake of spending premium currency, **Tuna Cans**, on character skins or construction. In the 2026 environment, efficiency is key. You must prioritize account utility over aesthetics to ensure you don't hit a progression wall.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Squad Deployment Bays">
    Purchase up to 5 Squad Deployment Bays (70 Cans each). This allows you to send multiple teams on resource explorations while your Mongoose Team farms main stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Storage Expansion">
    Expand your Character and Equipment slots. You will quickly find your inventory full of B-rank units and gear; having extra space prevents the "Inventory Full" error from stopping your auto-farm.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Black Book Usage">
    Save your **Black Books** (Instant Level 100). While it is tempting to use them on Miho, it is better to save them for late-game SS-rank units like **Invincible Dragon** who have much higher XP requirements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Gacha Banners">
Last Origin R+ is extremely F2P friendly. All units can be farmed or constructed using basic resources. Do not spend Tuna Cans on "Skin Gacha" banners; the rates are low, and it is always more cost-effective to buy a skin directly if you really want it.
</Callout>

</Section>
