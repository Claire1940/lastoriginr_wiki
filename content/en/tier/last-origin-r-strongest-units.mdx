One of the most common mistakes in Last Origin R is improper stat distribution. Even the **last origin r strongest units** will fail if they do not meet the "Endgame Thresholds." In 2026, the requirements for clearing high-level EX stages have become more stringent, particularly regarding Accuracy and Armor Penetration.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The 100% Critical Rule">
    Your primary damage dealers must reach 100% Critical Rate. This includes passive buffs from teammates and equipment. If your unit has 80% base Crit and a teammate provides a 20% buff, you are set. Excess Critical points are wasted, so transition those points into ATK.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Accuracy Barrier">
    Late-game enemies, especially in the 2026 patches, have high Evasion. Your DPS units should aim for 300% Accuracy. Without this, your most powerful attacks will simply miss, leading to team wipes in auto-farm scenarios.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Armor Penetration Requirements">
    Heavily armored bosses require at least 90% Armor Penetration. Use chips and specialized units like Bulgasari or Invincible Dragon to bypass enemy defenses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Never split stats randomly. A "jack-of-all-trades" unit is a master of none in Last Origin R. Specialize your units: DPS goes ATK/Crit, Tanks go DEF or EVA. Hybrid builds are almost never optimal for auto-farming.
</Callout>
