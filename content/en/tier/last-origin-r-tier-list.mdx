### Stat Allocation Guide
Adhering to specific stat thresholds is vital for all units to perform optimally. Ignoring these thresholds is a common reason why even good units might underperform, impacting your overall team's effectiveness.

*   **Damage Dealers (DPS)**: First, achieve **100% Critical Rate** (including passive skills and equipment chips). Once this threshold is met, allocate all remaining points into **ATK**.
*   **Evasion Tanks**: Maximize **Evasion**. Their primary survival mechanism is avoiding damage.
*   **Defense Tanks**: Split stat points between **DEF** and **HP** to ensure a balanced and durable frontline.

<Callout type="info">
Core stat thresholds for endgame: Critical Rate ≥100%, Accuracy ≥300%, Armor Penetration ≥90%. Aim for these as you progress.
</Callout>
