<Section icon={Target} title="Stat Allocation and Threshold Guide">

A common mistake when following a **last origin r character tier list** is ignoring the strict stat thresholds required for characters to function. Even an S-Tier unit will fail if their Accuracy or Critical Rate is neglected.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The 100% Critical Rule">
    Every Damage Dealer (DPS) must reach **100% Critical Rate**. This includes passive buffs from teammates and gear. Critical hits are not optional; they are the baseline for damage calculation in Last Origin R.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accuracy Benchmarks">
    For early game, 200% Accuracy is sufficient. However, as you move into Chapter 5 and beyond, you must aim for **300% Accuracy**. Missing an attack often means losing a unit to a counter-attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defensive Specialization">
    Never mix stats on tanks. An **Evasion Tank** should have 0 points in DEF/HP and 100% in Evasion. A **Defense Tank** should balance DEF and HP to maximize "Effective Health." Hybrid tanks usually fail at both roles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Equipment Tip">
Focus on upgrading your **β Chips** (ATK, CRIT, EVA) to +10 as your first priority. These provide the highest stat-per-resource return in the early game.
</Callout>
