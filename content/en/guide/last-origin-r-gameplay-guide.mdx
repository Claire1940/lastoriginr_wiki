<Section icon={Target} title="Efficient Resource Allocation & Stat Management">
Proper resource allocation and stat management are crucial for efficient progression in Last Origin R. New players start with valuable assets like approximately 300k resources, 2 Black Books, and a full set of β chips. Avoiding common pitfalls and utilizing these resources wisely will set you up for success.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Resource Management">
    - **Initial Resources**: Save for offline recovery and equipment enhancement. Avoid heavy construction early on.
    - **Black Books**: Extremely rare. Save for late-game meta units (e.g., No.85 Invincible Dragon). For early game, consider T-14 Miho or No.7 Scissors Lise.
    - **Equipment**: Upgrade free β chips (CRIT, ATK, EVA, SPD) to +10 first. Equip CRIT/ATK chips on Miho, transferring to Scissors later.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Stat Allocation Principles">

    - **Evasion Tanks**: Maximize **Evasion** for survival.
    - **Defense Tanks**: Split points between **DEF and HP** for balanced frontline durability.
    - **Thresholds**: Aim for Critical Rate ≥100%, Accuracy ≥300%, Armor Penetration ≥90% for effective combat.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering Combat & Tuna Can Usage">
Combat in Last Origin R utilizes a turn-based system, offering both auto/semi-auto and manual playstyles. For beginners, the **auto/semi-auto** modes are perfect for AFK farming and clearing early floors, allowing your teams to fight independently. Manual combat, while unlocking full team potential, requires practice and is best approached after mastering the basics.

| Combat Style | Description                                   | Ideal Use Case              |
| :----------- | :-------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| **Auto/Semi-Auto** | Teams fight independently without manual skill control. | Early floors, AFK resource farming, casual play. |
| **Manual**   | Active choice of skill order and target priorities. | Tough endgame content, boss fights, maximizing team potential. |

Before tackling serious combat, ensure your units meet basic requirements: level 90, fully linked, with +10 SS gear for key damage dealers. Crucial stat thresholds like Critical Rate ≥100%, Accuracy ≥300%, and Armor Penetration ≥90% are vital for success.

<Callout type="info" title="Tuna Cans: Premium Currency Priority">
**Tuna Cans** are the premium currency in Last Origin R, primarily used for account storage and team slot expansion. Your top priority should be buying **Expanded Squad Deployment Bays** (70 cans each) until you have 5 teams for resource exploration. After that, invest in additional **character slots** and **equipment slots**. Avoid waiting room slots as you only need to keep S/SS characters and SS gear, and regular inventory clearing prevents overflow.
</Callout>
