<Section icon={Swords} title="Last Origin R: How to Play the Grid Combat System">

The core of the gameplay revolves around a unique turn system and a 3x3 grid stage. Learning **last origin r how to play** effectively requires mastering the spatial relationship between your units and the enemy. Each unit has a specific attack range and area of effect (AoE), making their placement on the grid the most important decision you make before the battle begins.

<Callout type="warning" title="Turn Priority">
Individual speed stats determine the turn order. Fast units can sometimes act twice before a slow enemy moves once.
</Callout>

| Role | Grid Positioning | Primary Objective |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Defender** | Front Row | Absorb damage and protect allies |
| **Attacker** | Middle/Back Row | Eliminate high-threat targets |
| **Support** | Back Row | Provide buffs and energy recharge |

Combat is not just about attacking; it is about managing Action Points (AP). Every skill consumes a specific amount of AP. If a unit ends a turn with high AP, they will move sooner in the next round. This creates a dynamic flow where you must decide between using a powerful, expensive skill now or waiting for a more opportunistic moment.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Building Your Perfect Squad">

Squad composition in Last Origin R is limited to five units placed within the nine available grid slots. A successful formation maximizes the "Link" bonuses and passive skills that units provide to their neighbors. Some units grant defensive shields to those directly behind them, while others increase the critical hit rate of adjacent allies.

<Callout type="info" title="Formation Buffs">
Check the "Range" and "Area" of passive skills in the unit profile. Some buffs only trigger if the unit is in a specific row.
</Callout>

| Archetype | Composition | Best Use Case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Tank/Buster** | 2 Defenders, 3 Attackers | High-difficulty boss fights |
| **Speed Blitz** | 1 Defender, 4 Fast Attackers | Farming low-level materials |
| **Balanced** | 1 Tank, 2 Attackers, 2 Supports | General campaign progression |

Strategic formations allow even lower-rarity units to outperform legendary ones if their skills complement the team. For instance, an AGS-focused squad benefits significantly from specialized robotic supports that provide unique energy efficiency buffs unavailable to Bio-roids.

Growth in Last Origin R involves more than just gaining experience points. Furthermore, equipment plays a vital role in fine-tuning a unit's performance, such as increasing their accuracy to counter high-evasion enemies.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficient Leveling">
Use the "Auto-Battle" feature on previously cleared stages to level up new units safely while you focus on other tasks.
