### Resource Allocation Priorities
New players start with valuable resources like 300k resources, 2 Black Books, and a full set of β chips. Proper allocation of these early assets is crucial to avoid common pitfalls and accelerate your progress.

| Resource Type | Early Game Priority | Late Game Strategy | Notes |
| :------------ | :------------------ | :----------------- | :---- |
| **Resources** | Offline recovery, Equipment enhancement | Heavy construction (once stable) | Avoid early heavy construction; it's inefficient. |
| **Black Books** | T-14 Miho, No.7 Scissors Lise (if needed) | No.85 Invincible Dragon, other meta units | Instantly max level/skills; save for top-tier units. |
| **β Chips** | Upgrade free chips to +10 (CRIT, ATK, EVA, SPD) | Transfer CRIT/ATK chips to endgame DPS | Essential for early stat boosts. |
| **Cores** | Blue common cores for early links | Gold rare cores for top-tier endgame units (No.2 Lavata, No.85 Invincible Dragon) | Gold cores are extremely limited; use wisely. |

### Stat Allocation Rules for Upgrades
Proper stat allocation is vital for maximizing your units' potential. Follow these general rules for all unit types:

| Unit Type       | Primary Stat Focus | Secondary Stat Focus | Notes                                         |
| :-------------- | :----------------- | :------------------- | :-------------------------------------------- |
| **Damage Dealers (DPS)** | 100% Critical Rate (including passives/chips) | All remaining points into ATK | Crucial for consistent damage output.           |
| **Evasion Tanks** | Max Evasion        | -                    | Their survival depends entirely on high evasion. |
| **Defense Tanks** | Balanced DEF and HP | -                    | Ensures balanced frontline durability.           |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Stat Threshold Mistakes">
Ignoring critical stat thresholds like 100% Critical Rate, 300% Accuracy, or 90% Armor Penetration can cause even well-built teams to fail in harder content. Always ensure your units meet these minimums.
</Callout>
