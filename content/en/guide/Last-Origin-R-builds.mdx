<Section icon={Target} title="Strategic Resource Allocation for Optimal Builds">
Efficient resource management is paramount for developing strong Last Origin R builds. New players begin with a limited pool of resources, Black Books, and β chips. Using these wisely will prevent early-game setbacks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Tuna Cans Spending">
    Tuna Cans are the premium currency. Prioritize **Expanded Squad Deployment Bays** (70 cans each) to field more teams for resource exploration. Aim for 5 total deployment slots. After that, acquire additional **character slots** and **equipment slots**. Avoid spending Cans on gacha skins early on.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Conserve Black Books">
    Black Books instantly max a unit's level and skills. Save them for late-game meta units like **No.85 Invincible Dragon**. If you must use one early, consider **T-14 Miho** or **No.7 Scissors Lise**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Equipment Judiciously">
    Upgrade your free β chips (CRIT, ATK, EVA, SPD) to **+10** first. Equip CRIT/ATK chips on Miho, then transfer them to Scissors when you acquire her later. Don't waste resources on low-tier gear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Cores Wisely">
    Use **blue common cores** freely for early links. **Gold rare cores** are extremely limited; reserve them for top-tier endgame units such as **No.2 Lavata** and **No.85 Invincible Dragon**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Resource Pitfall">
Never dump all your initial resources into heavy construction. This is an inefficient and risky practice that can severely hinder your early game progression and the development of your Last Origin R builds.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Stat Allocation Guide for Diverse Last Origin R Builds">
Proper stat allocation is critical for maximizing unit performance in any Last Origin R build. Follow these general rules to ensure your units are optimized for their roles.

| Unit Role       | Primary Stat Priority           | Secondary Stat Priority | Notes                                                |
|-----------------|---------------------------------|-------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| **Damage Dealers** | **100% Critical Rate** (with passives/chips) | Max ATK                 | Crit is paramount, then raw damage.                  |
| **Evasion Tanks** | **Max Evasion**                 | -                       | Survival depends entirely on high evasion.           |
| **Defense Tanks** | **Balanced DEF/HP**             | -                       | Distribute points to ensure balanced durability.     |

<Callout type="info">
Accuracy is crucial for all units, especially damage dealers. Aim for at least **300% Accuracy** to consistently hit enemies, particularly in later stages. Armor Penetration of **90%** is also a key threshold for DPS units against armored foes.
</Callout>
