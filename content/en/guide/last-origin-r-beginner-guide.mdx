### Early Game Stat Allocation Priorities

Proper stat allocation is crucial for maximizing your units' effectiveness. Follow these general guidelines for different unit roles:

*   **Damage Dealers (DPS)**: First, achieve **100% Critical Rate** (including passives and chips). Once capped, allocate **all remaining points into ATK**.
*   **Evasion Tanks**: Maximize **Evasion**. Their survival directly depends on dodging incoming attacks.
*   **Defense Tanks**: Split points between **DEF and HP** to create a balanced and durable frontline.

<Section icon={BookOpen} title="Combat Fundamentals for New Players">
Combat in Last Origin R is turn-based, offering two main playstyles: **Auto/Semi-Auto** and **Manual**. For beginners, **Auto/Semi-Auto** modes are your best friends. They allow your teams to fight independently without manual skill control, making it perfect for AFK farming and clearing early stages easily.

**Manual combat** requires active skill selection and target prioritization. While it unlocks your team's full potential for tough endgame content, it demands practice and understanding. As a new player, stick to auto modes until you've mastered the basics and built strong core teams.

### Essential Combat Requirements

Before tackling challenging content, ensure your units meet these basic thresholds:

*   **Level 90** fully linked units.
*   **+10 SS gear** for your key damage dealers.
*   Core stats at minimum thresholds:
    *   **Critical Rate ≥100%**
    *   **Accuracy ≥300%**
    *   **Armor Penetration ≥90%**

<Callout type="info">
Ignoring these stat thresholds is a common reason for team failures. Copying guides without matching the required gear and stats will often lead to frustrating wipes.
</Callout>
