Efficient resource allocation is paramount for new players in Last Origin R. You start with valuable assets like 300k resources, 2 Black Books, and a full set of β chips. Avoiding common mistakes in their usage will accelerate your progress.

  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resources">

  <Step num="4" title="Cores">

  </Step>
