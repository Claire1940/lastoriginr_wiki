<Section icon={Target} title="Efficient Resource Allocation and Management">
Proper resource management is paramount in Last Origin R. New players start with valuable assets like approximately **300k resources**, **2 Black Books**, and a full set of β chips. Avoiding wasteful spending is key to a smooth **Last Origin R progression**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Blind Spending">
Never dump all your resources into heavy construction early on. It's often inefficient and risky, potentially hindering your long-term progress. Prioritize offline recovery and equipment enhancement.
</Callout>

### Key Resource Prioritization

| Resource       | Early Game Use                                   | Late Game / Priority Use                       | Avoid                                           |
| :------------- | :----------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------- |
| **Resources**  | Offline recovery, equipment enhancement          | Advanced construction, high-tier upgrades      | Heavy construction early game                   |
| **Black Books** | T-14 Miho or No.7 Scissors Lise (if necessary)   | **Late-game meta units** (e.g., No.85 Invincible Dragon) | Random units, non-meta characters               |
| **β Chips**    | Upgrade CRIT/ATK/EVA/SPD to +10, equip on Miho   | Transfer to top-tier DPS like Scissors Lise    | Ignoring upgrades, using inefficient chips      |

### Combat Playstyles and Progression

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start with Auto/Semi-Auto">
    For new players, **Auto/Semi-Auto** modes are perfect for AFK farming and clearing early stages. Your teams will fight independently without manual skill control, allowing you to focus on team building and resource management.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Core Mechanics">
    Before attempting manual combat, ensure your units are **level 90**, fully linked, and equipped with +10 SS gear for key damage dealers. Understand basic counter rules (e.g., tribal teams vs. Glutton/Centurion, fire damage vs. ice bosses).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Transition to Manual for Endgame">
    **Manual combat** unlocks full team potential but requires active skill timing and target prioritization. Practice is essential for tough endgame content and specific boss fights. Gradually introduce manual control as you become more comfortable with your team's abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>
