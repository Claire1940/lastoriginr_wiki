### Farming Locations for Beginners
To "Link" your units (increasing their stats without leveling), you need duplicate copies. Focus your energy on these specific stages:
- **Stage 3-8**: Farm extra copies of **T-14 Miho** to reach 500% Link bonus.
- **Stage 5-8**: Farm for **X05**, a premier DPS unit that transitions you into the mid-game.
- **Stage 5-8EX**: This is where you will eventually farm high-tier SS equipment and chips.

<Section icon={Target} title="Stat Allocation and Character Building Rules">

Stat management is where most new players fail. In **last origin r**, dumping points randomly into stats will lead to "wiping" on stages even if your level is high. Each role has a specific mathematical threshold they must meet to be effective in 2026 meta content.

| Stat Type | Requirement | Priority | Reasoning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical Rate** | **100%** | **Highest** | Essential for triggering DPS passives and maximizing damage. |
| **Accuracy** | **300% - 350%** | High | Prevents "Miss" results against high-evasion late-game enemies. |
| **Evasion** | **Max (All Points)** | High | Evasion tanks die instantly if they take a single hit. |
| **Armor Pen** | **90% - 100%** | Medium | Necessary to bypass high-defense bosses in Chapter 7+. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Accuracy Warning">
If your damage dealer has 100% Crit but only 150% Accuracy, you will fail to hit scouts and wasps in later stages. Always balance Accuracy before dumping the rest into ATK.
</Callout>

**Stat Point Distribution Guide:**
- **Damage Dealers (Miho/Bulgasari)**: Calculate your passive buffs first. If a passive gives 20% Crit, you only need 80% from points/chips. Once at 100% Crit and 250%+ Accuracy, put all remaining points into **ATK**.
- **Defense Tanks (Steel Draco)**: Split points between **HP and DEF**. In the early game, raw HP is often more valuable than DEF as many enemies have armor-piercing capabilities.
- **Evasion Tanks (Pinto)**: Put **100% of points into Evasion**. Do not touch HP or DEF; their survival depends entirely on not being touched.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Follow these steps to transition from a new recruit to an efficient farmer within your first week of play.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear Tactical Training">
    Complete all missions in the Tactical Training Room. This provides the full **Mongoose Team**, level-up materials, and your first set of SS equipment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Push Main Story to 3-8">
    Use the Mongoose Team to reach stage 3-8. Stop here and set up an auto-farm to get duplicates of **T-14 Miho**. Link her to 500% to boost her base stats significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Gear with Beta Chips">
    Upgrade the free **Beta Chips** (ATK, Crit, EVA) given at the start of the game to **+10**. These are foundational items that remain useful even in the endgame.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock All Deployment Squads">
    Use your initial Tuna Cans to unlock 5 squads. Send 4 squads on **Resource Exploration** missions 24/7 to ensure you never run out of components or nutrients.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Push to Chapter 5-8">
    With your linked Miho and +10 gear, push to Chapter 5. Farm stage 5-8 for **X05** and begin building your second specialized team for harder content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Offline Farming">
